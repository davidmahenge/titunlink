About Our Company

Titun Link Enterprise brings over seven years of expertise in supplying high-quality mining consumables. Our commitment to reliability, efficiency, and customer satisfaction has established us as a trusted partner in the mining industry.

Our main branch, strategically located in Katoro, Geita, has successfully met the demands of clients in Lwamgasa, Nyarugusu, Katoro, Kakola, Kahama, and other areas within the Lake Zone. We are committed to expanding our operations across key mining regions in Tanzania and Southern Africa, further enhancing our reach and service delivery.

Explosives

Our explosives are sourced from reputable manufacturers, ensuring compliance with international safety standards and regulations. We prioritize safety and reliability in all our explosive products, providing our clients with the confidence they need for their mining and demolition projects.

We provide safe, regulated explosives for mining, construction, and demolition, adhering to strict safety standards and legal compliance. Count on us for reliable solutions and expert support.

We specialize in importing a diverse range of explosive products, including but not limited to..

  • EXPLOGEL: A versatile and safe explosive gel for controlled blasting.
  • NONELI: Non-electric detonators for reliable and safe initiation.
  • Detonating Fuses: High-quality fuses for precise timing in blasting operations.

Chemicals

Our chemicals are sourced from reputable suppliers, ensuring compliance with international safety standards and regulations. We prioritize quality and reliability in all our chemical products, providing our clients with the confidence they need for their mining and industrial processes.

We are committed to sustainability and environmental responsibility, ensuring that our chemical products are safe for both the environment and human health. We work closely with our clients to ensure that they have access to the best chemical solutions that meet their operational needs while minimizing environmental impact.

Our chemicals are available in various forms and quantities, allowing us to cater to the specific requirements of our clients. Whether you need bulk chemicals for large-scale operations or smaller quantities for specialized applications, we have the right solutions for you.

High-quality chemicals including Sodium Cyanide, Ammonium Nitrate, Caustic Soda, Mercury, and more.

  • Sodium Cyanide: Essential for gold extraction and other mining processes.
  • Ammonium Nitrate: Used in fertilizers and as an explosive in mining operations.
  • Caustic Soda: A versatile chemical used in various industrial applications.
  • Mercury: Used in gold processing and other chemical applications.
